About This Project

Besorah Yeshua (Gospel of Jesus) is a Bible reading planner designed to help you stay consistent in daily Scripture reading.

Why Free? We believe everyone should have access to Bible reading tools. This free tier provides all essential features without any cost, forever.

Privacy First: Your data stays on your device. No account required, no tracking, no data collection. Your Bible reading journey is completely private.

Open Source: The free tier is open source. Developers can contribute or build upon it for their communities.

Ethiopian Calendar: We honor the ancient Ethiopian Orthodox tradition with a dedicated calendar plan following traditional readings and feast days.

Built with love for the global body of Christ. Use it freely and share with others.
