About This Project

Besorah Yeshua (Gospel of Jesus) is a Bible reading planner designed to help you stay consistent in daily Scripture reading.

Why Two Tiers? We believe everyone should have access to Bible reading tools. Our free tier provides essential features for everyone, while our Basic Tier ($3/month) supports development and provides enhanced features for serious students.

Privacy First: Your data stays on your device in free tier. Basic Tier uses secure cloud storage with encryption.

Open Source: The free tier is open source. Developers can contribute or build upon it.

Support: Basic Tier subscriptions help maintain and improve the tool for everyone.

Built with love for the global body of Christ. Use it freely and share with others.
